The Philippine National Police

Welcome to the official portal of the Philippine National Police. Explore the programs, divisions, and career opportunities available within the organization.

Divisions and Organizations

Training Service

Responsible for developing and conducting training programs for police personnel.

Philippine National Police Academy

The primary academy responsible for producing the officer core of PNP

Headquarters Support Service

Provides administrative and logistical support to PNP headquarters.

Highway Patrol Group

Ensures road safety and enforces traffic laws nationwide.

Criminal Investigation and Detection Group

Handles investigations of major crimes and assists police operations.

Internal Affairs Service

Maintains discipline and investigates misconduct within the PNP.

Intelligence Group

Conducts intelligence gathering and security threat monitoring.

Special Action Force

An elite unit tasked with counter-terrorism and high-risk operations.

Special Weapons and Tactics

Handles hostage rescue, armed confrontations, and tactical missions.

Aviation Unit

Provides aerial surveillance, transport, and aviation support.

Department of the Interior and Local Government

Supervises and administers the Philippine National Police.

Department of Foreign Affairs

Manages international relations and cooperation with foreign agencies.

Recruitment Process

  1. Join the official group
  2. Report to Camp Crame
  3. Complete Basic Police Training
  4. Apply or try out for specialized divisions
